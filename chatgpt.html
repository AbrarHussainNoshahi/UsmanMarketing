Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before choosing your plan.

What is the difference between the Semi-Private and Private GPT-4 plans?

The Semi-Private Plan offers access to ChatGPT Plus (GPT-4) on 1 device only with shared login credentials.

The Private Plan gives you a fully private account on your own email with access on multiple devices, full control over billing, chat history, memory, and tools.

Can I use the Semi-Private Plan on more than one device?

No. The Semi-Private Plan is locked to one device only. Switching devices will void the subscription. If you need flexibility across devices, go for the Private Plan.

Do I get access to ChatGPT Plus and GPT-4 with both plans?

Yes, both plans include access to ChatGPT Plus with GPT-4. The difference lies in account control, device usage, and personalization features.

Is there any replacement or support if the login stops working?

Yes, both plans come with a replacement guarantee during the validity period. If the subscription stops working or you face login issues, a replacement will be provided.

Who should choose the Private Plan over the Semi-Private one?

Choose the Private Plan if you:

   - Need to use GPT-4 on multiple devices

   - Want full control over your account settings, memory, billing, and tools

   - Rely on GPT-4 for daily content creation, development, or research

The Semi-Private Plan is ideal for individuals testing GPT-4 or using it on one secure device for basic tasks.
